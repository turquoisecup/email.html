HISPANIC HERITAGE MONTH ASSEMBLY ON FRIDAY

Just a reminder that we are inviting all Fashion Families to the school this Friday, September 27th for our Hispanic Heritage Month Assembly.  The Assembly will take place three times during the day and you are invited to any of the performances.

12th graders will see the performance during Period 3 that takes place from 9:50 am to 10:40 am

11th graders will see the performance during Period 6 that takes place from 12:25 pm to 1:10 pm

9th & 10th graders will see the performance during Period 8 that takes place from 2:05 pm to 2:50 pm

Our Student Government thinks you will really enjoy the assembly!
HSFI SUCCESS SKILLS TEAMS - STUDENT INTEREST

I wanted to let you and all Fashion Families know that I am still looking for student volunteers for our Success Skill Teams.  We are creating teams where students and staff come together to develop our Success Skills - the skills we have identified that lead to success in high school, work, college and life - Be KIND / Be CREATIVE / Be an effective COMMUNICATOR / Be a constructive PROBLEM-SOLVER / WORK HARD / Be DEPENDABLE / Embrace TECHNOLOGY / Be an INFORMED and CONTRIBUTING member of society.

Please message me on Jupiter or send me an email at dblank@schools.nyc.gov if you are interested in joining these teams that are meeting this coming Monday.
SECURITY UPDATES

Just a reminder that it is important for you and all Fashion Families to know that the NYC Department of Education does random scanning of students for weapons and this scanning could happen any day.  The most common confiscated item is pepper spray which some families provide to you as a safety measure in traveling along the subways.  We understand why students and families carry such an item for safety, but understand that the NYC Department of Education has zero tolerance for any sort of weapons in schools.
